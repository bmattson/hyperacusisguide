About Hyperacusis Guide

Hyperacusis Guide was created to help people with hyperacusis by sharing advice we consider safe and most likely to prevent symptoms from worsening. The guidance is based on our own experiences living with hyperacusis, noxacusis, and tinnitus. We also aim to clarify misleading information that is often found online and within the medical community. For those newly affected, finding reliable resources to understand their condition, manage symptoms safely, and make informed choices about treatment and daily life can be especially challenging.

Our goal is to provide clear and practical guidance that helps anyone living with hyperacusis manage their condition safely and improve their chances of recovery.

Disclaimer: The information on this website is based on our personal experiences and opinions. It is for informational purposes only and should not replace evaluation or treatment by a qualified medical professional.
